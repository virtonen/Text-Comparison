Instructions

  1. Paste or type your original text into the left textarea, or upload a PDF file.
  2. Paste or type your modified text into the right textarea, or upload a PDF file.
  3. Click the "Compare" button to see the differences.
  4. For PDFs, differences will be highlighted directly on the rendered documents.
